About me

Researcher and Engineer with experience at multiple companies in both Machine Learning and Blockchain. My academic background is in Mathematics (First class honours BSc) and I possess a MSc in Computational Finance (from the UCL Computer Science department). I have worked for/with numerous companies in the ML/Blockchain spaces. My roles have involved leading teams in high pressure, result orientated environments as well as having been part of teams containing some of the Brightest minds in the London tech scene.

Herein lies some notable examples of work I have produced whilst in industry and academia.

Other demonstrations of my skills include international recognition of projects I have lead development on, for example, by the UN and my success quickly climbing ranks in the companies I have worked for (see experience below).
